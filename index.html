The problem we're solving

Influencer marketing is broken
for everyone

Brands spend thousands on posts that flop. Creators spend hours negotiating deals that go nowhere. There's a better way — and it's already working in the US.

For Brands

You're paying for promises, not results

Traditional influencer marketing charges per post or per follower. A creator with 100k followers might get 200 views. You've already paid. There's no refund.

For Creators

You're undervalued unless you're already famous

Without a huge following, brands won't talk to you. You spend more time pitching than creating. When you land a deal, negotiating fair pay is exhausting.

The old model

Agencies take 30–50%. ROI is unmeasured.

You have no idea if your £5,000 campaign generated £500 or £50,000 in value. Performance is unmeasured. Accountability is non-existent.

For micro-creators

Great content. No path to income.

You make content people love. But without a massive following, there's no clear way to monetise it. TikTok's creator fund barely covers coffee.
